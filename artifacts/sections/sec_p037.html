The catechising of the young, the personal improvement of memorizing Scripture, the study of the original languages, the daily consecration of self to God and his service, and many other such things, are matters which cannot be put off one day without loss. They should be understood and entered upon at once in order to yield their full benefits. Even little matters, that might easily be overlooked, will have great influence upon the whole of the minister’s life. His work is so unspeakably momentous, for the glory of God, for the welfare of souls and for his own happiness, that its very first hours should be filled with the greatest efficiency.
