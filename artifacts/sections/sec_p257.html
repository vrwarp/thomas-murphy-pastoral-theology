It may take a long time before the inquirer is brought to the peace of Christ; sometimes the process is very slow and discouraging. But the heart is reached at last, and it is often the case that those who struggle long and almost hopelessly are the most decided when at last they are brought into the clear light of the Sun of Righteousness.
